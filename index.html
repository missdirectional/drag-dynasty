The Blueprint

This app will change how people interact with drag. By hosting a creator marketplace, performer live streams, weekly competitions and more in one space, It enables absolute independence for drag artists to capture non-sweaty dollar bills and scale their drag career.

Hub 01 // Ecosystem

The Networq

Our decentralized gig marketplace. Connect instantly with videographers, makeup artists, wig designers, beats-makers, and producers built specifically to collaborate on drag royalty standards.

Hub 02 // Currency

Bling 💎

The sovereign in-app digital currency. Fans load up and tip performers in real-time with zero friction. Instant value, direct transfers, completely bypassing predatory traditional payment processors.

Hub 03 // Patronage

Castles 🏰

Build your digital stronghold. Castles allow fans to subscribe directly to performers for exclusive backstage access, custom media vaults (powered by Mux), and private royal feeds.

Hub 04 // Quests

Drops 💰

The interactive request board. Fans drop bounties or custom challenges with Bling/$$$, and performers complete them to claim the bounty. Direct engagement, monetized instantly.
